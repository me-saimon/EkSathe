By Morshed Alam
calendar_today May 12, 2026
location_on Dhaka, Bangladesh

The Story

Rahim is a vibrant 5-year-old boy from a modest neighborhood in Dhaka. Two months ago, he was diagnosed with a congenital heart defect that requires immediate surgical correction. His father, a small-scale vendor, and his mother, a homemaker, have exhausted all their savings on diagnostic tests and initial care.

The doctors at the National Heart Institute have scheduled the surgery for early next month, but the family still needs ৳ 1,80,000 to cover the remaining hospital and post-operative costs. Without this surgery, Rahim's condition will deteriorate rapidly, affecting his ability to walk or even play like other children.

EkSathe has independently verified all medical documents, prescription records, and hospital estimates. We are calling upon our community to come forward and help give Rahim a second chance at life. Every Taka counts.
